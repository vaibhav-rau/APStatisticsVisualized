Overview

Unit 1 focuses on exploring one-variable data, including types of data, graphical displays, measures of center and spread, and comparing distributions. This is inteded to be an introduction into the world of statistics, just before you start helping design a new plane.

Key Topics

  • Types of data: categorical vs. numerical
  • Graphical displays: histograms, box plots, dot plots
  • Measures of center: mean and median
  • Measures of spread: range, interquartile range, standard deviation
  • Summary statistics: percentiles, z-scores, outliers
  • Comparing distributions: centers, spreads, shapes
