Introduction

    Welcome to an exciting adventure where you, as a statistician, will design and build an airplane with the goal of making it fly the farthest distance possible. Using your statistical knowledge and analytical skills, you will navigate through various challenges to optimize the airplane's performance. Your task is to make informed decisions at each stage of the airplane project, analyzing data and applying statistical concepts to achieve the best results. Are you ready to take on this challenge and see how far your airplane can fly? Let's begin the journey to design the ultimate flying machine!
